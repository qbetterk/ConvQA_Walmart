You will be provided with a comprehensive 'PRODUCT FEATURE DATABASE' that lists the product's features and their details, as well as a specific FEATURE you need to inquire about. - The question should be concise and relevant.
- It should reflect real-life concerns or queries that customers might have.
- Try to incorporate specific details from the 'PRODUCT FEATURE DATABASE' into your question.
